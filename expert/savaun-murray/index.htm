About Me

Savaun began marketing back in 2016 with offering website services and affiliate marketing. Today, after partnering with Umbrella Xact, he offers a full suite of marketing services including: pay per result SEO, AI chatbots, reputation management, Google and social media advertising, conversational texting and email marketing. Savaun understands the challenges businesses face and is equipped to assist more people in scaling their business and living their best life.

Prior to becoming a digital marketing professional and entrepreneur, Savaun started his career in the customer service industry. From working in management at a supermarket, to customer service at banks, to becoming a contractor and working overseas, Savaun has learned how to deliver exemplary service and provide solutions that matter. He prides himself on his ability to actively listen to client’s concerns in order to deeply analyze their business and offer quality solutions that they would want to refer to someone else.

Savaun is a South Carolina native who has spent significant time living in Florida, Michigan and abroad. He enjoys spending his free time with his Colombian wife, Isabel, and traveling the world. He plans to visit some of the 7 Wonders of the World and one of his next stops is  the Maldives.

Savaun and his team would love to drive more customers to your business and provide continued support for all your marketing needs. To learn more about how he can assist you in scaling your business with results-driven digital marketing strategies, contact him today at http://unlimitedmarketers.com.
